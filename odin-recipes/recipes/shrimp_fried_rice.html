Description

This is a shrimp fried rice recipe copied from allrecipes.com. We will learn how to make shrimp fried rice, restaurant-style with this recipe.

The recipe is by OKBAT, and is how the author used to cook shrimp fried rice when the author's father still owned a restaurant.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups water
  • 1½ cups uncooked white rice
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup fresh bean sprouts
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 1½ cups cooked medium shrimp peeled and deveined without tail
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • ¼cup chopped green onion
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan
  3. Heat a large skillet or wok for 2 minutes
  4. Mix in cooled rice and shrimp and cook for another 3 mins
  5. Add in eggs, green onion, soy sauce, salt, pepper, and sesame oil
